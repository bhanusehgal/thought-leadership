### Voice Summary
The voice is analytical, grounded in bank operations, and structured to reveal hidden system risk. It avoids storytelling and opinion theater, then reframes common compliance assumptions into broader liquidity and balance-sheet implications. The stance is practitioner-level: precise, risk-aware, and operationally literate.

- Systems thinking across compliance, liquidity, and transaction behavior.
- Failure analysis through assumption stress-testing.
- Signal extraction mindset (looking for early indicators before lagging metrics move).
- Causal framing over surface description.
- Operational realism (focus on what institutions actually do and where it breaks).
